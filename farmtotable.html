Farm to Table Dinners

More than ever, we are disconnected from where our food comes from. What better way is there to learn more about the food we eat than to visit the farm and see how it is grown? Box 12 Farms is a centennial farm in central Illinois that is now offering the community to tour the farm while enjoying delicous food prepared by some amazing chefs. Our tours run from April to November. It includes afternoon of learning and enjoying the fruits of the land. The Farm to Table dinners allow the chance to meet the farm families behind the products, see our animals first-hand, and ask questions about raising and processing livestock. The afternoon will start out with a snack and beverages after all the guests arrive. Following our snack, we will begin the farm tour. Guests will have the opportunity to ask questions, hold baby chicks, gather fresh eggs, and interact with our goats. Meals are taken on the veranda. All food is prepared in a separate barn (not used by our livestock). In case of inclement weather, the dinner will served in this barn. The barn can also be rented out for private events.

The experience runs from 4 - 8 PM. Tickets are $70.00 a person. Lemonade and water will be provided. Craft beers from local breweries such as Hand of Fate, Anvil and Forge, and Buzz Bomb will be availabe for purchase, as well as a selection of wine, either by glass or bottle. Children are welcome as long as they are accompanied and supervised by an adult. All ticket sales are non-refundable. If you cannot attend the event anymore, you may transfer your tickets to another person .
